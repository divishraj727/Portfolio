# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Recruiters, hiring managers, and startup founders who land here from Google searches ("Divish Raj", "full stack developer portfolio") or from Divish's LinkedIn/WhatsApp shares. Most arrive on a mid-range Android phone, skim for 30 seconds, and decide whether to open the resume or the projects. A second audience: fellow developers judging craft.

## Product Purpose

The personal portfolio of Divish Raj O (full stack developer, Bangalore: Django + React + AWS/K8s). It must (1) rank on Google for his name and portfolio keywords, (2) convert a skim into an email or LinkedIn contact, and (3) prove engineering skill through its own behavior: the site itself is the demo. Success = interview requests and project enquiries.

## Brand Personality

Orbital, engineered, luminous. The feeling of a precision instrument running in deep space: calm confidence, not loud sci-fi. The visitor's first reaction should be "how was this made?", the second should be "this person ships".

## Anti-references

- The site's own previous skin (cream + terracotta gauravsaxena.site clone): retired, do not drift back.
- Neon-cyan hologram / synthwave-purple "futuristic" portfolios: the saturated AI lane for this brief.
- Template portfolios: identical card grids, icon-heading-text repetition, hero-metric blocks.
- Sci-fi costume typography (Orbitron-style chrome lettering, fake HUD noise everywhere).

## Design Principles

1. **The site is the proof.** Every choice demonstrates the engineering it claims: speed, precision, working interactivity.
2. **Spectacle that ships.** Effects carry a budget: smooth on a mid-range phone, deferred loading, static fallback. No effect is worth a janky frame.
3. **Content outlives the shader.** SEO copy, semantic HTML, and structured data render with JS disabled; WebGL is enhancement, never a gate.
4. **One world, not widgets.** A single aurora-on-black world spans hero to footer; sections vary in treatment, not in universe.
5. **Touch is the primary input.** Scroll and tap interactions are designed for thumbs first, mouse second.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA. Body text ≥4.5:1 against the dark surface. Full `prefers-reduced-motion` alternative (static scene, instant transitions). All interactive elements reachable by keyboard; canvas is decorative with text alternatives.
